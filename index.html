How to Play

Select a mode, choose which tables (1–20) you want, and a max factor (for example “up to 11” means 1–11). The game runs automatically.

Multiplication: each factor is shown inside a card with its blocks. After a correct answer, you’ll see that many groups, each containing the second factor as blocks.

Division: the problem uses a circle cut into slices equal to the divisor. After a correct answer, each slice is filled and labelled with the quotient.

Type the answer and press Enter or click Submit. After feedback and visuals, press Enter again or click Next for the next fact.
